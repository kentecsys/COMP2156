COMP 3104 - DevOps is a course at George Brown College that introduces students to DevOps principles, tools, and practices. 
DevOps improves collaboration between software development and IT operations, ensuring faster and more efficient software delivery.

Key Topics Covered in COMP 3104:
- Version Control (Git, GitHub)
- Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD)
- Configuration Management (Ansible, Puppet, Chef)
- Cloud and Virtualization (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Monitoring and Logging

The course provides hands-on experience with DevOps tools to prepare students for real-world software development and IT environments.
